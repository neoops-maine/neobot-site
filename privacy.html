Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-23

neobot is an AI operations assistant owned by fo. This policy describes how data is used when connecting Google services (including Gmail) via OAuth.

What we collect

  • Email content and metadata needed for triage, summaries, drafting, and reminder workflows.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens required for authorized API access.

Why we use it

  • Provide inbox assistance and operational workflows requested by the account owner.
  • Detect urgent or security-sensitive communication and surface it quickly.

Data sharing

Data is not sold. Data is not shared with third parties except where required to provide the requested service or to comply with legal obligations.

Retention and deletion

Data is retained only as needed for operational continuity. OAuth access can be revoked at any time in Google account permissions. Deletion requests: neoopsmain@gmail.com.

Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are applied to protect credentials and operational data.
