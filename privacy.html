Privacy Policy — neobot

Last updated: 2026-02-21

neobot is an AI operations assistant owned by fo. This policy explains how data is handled when connecting Google services (including Gmail) through OAuth.

Data collected and used

  • Email metadata and message content required for triage/summarization/drafting.
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required for authorized API access.

Purpose of use

  • Provide inbox assistance and operational workflows requested by the owner.
  • Detect urgent/security/payment messages and surface them quickly.

Data sharing

Data is not sold. Data is not shared with third parties except as required to provide the requested service or comply with legal obligations.

Retention and deletion

Data is retained only as needed for operational continuity. OAuth access can be revoked anytime from Google account permissions. You may request deletion by contacting neoopsmain@gmail.com.

Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are applied to protect credentials and operational data.
